Know about

Times New Xu

The idea of this project title, “Times New Xu” comes from “Times New Roman”, the most widely used font in academic materials. And the replaced “Xu” here stands for Prof.Yangsheng Xu, a famous robotics scientist, a great calligrapher, and also the founding president of CUHK(SZ). Our idea is to collect existing Xu’s calligraphy works, and then train a nerual network to study it. By using our software, users can define the context and kind (such as Couplets) of calligraphy, then they will own a special Xu’s calligraphy work.
